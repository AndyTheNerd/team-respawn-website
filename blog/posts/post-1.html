Top 5 Games to Play this Summer

Published on July 25, 2024 by Jane Doe

Summer is the perfect time to catch up on your backlog or discover a new world to get lost in. Whether you're a fan of epic fantasy, fast-paced action, or relaxing indie experiences, there's something for everyone. We've compiled a list of our top 5 recommendations that will keep you entertained all season long.

1. Starfield

Bethesda's latest epic takes you on a journey through the stars. With a universe filled with over 1,000 planets to explore, a deep character customization system, and endless quests, you'll be spending a lot of time exploring the cosmos. The freedom to create your own story is unparalleled, making it a must-play for RPG fans.

2. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

The sequel to Breath of the Wild expands on everything that made its predecessor great. New abilities like Ultrahand and Fuse open up incredible creative possibilities, allowing you to build vehicles, weapons, and contraptions that will break the laws of physics. It's a game that constantly rewards your curiosity.

3. Elden Ring

The game that defined a genre. FromSoftware's open-world masterpiece is still a fantastic experience, especially now that the community has discovered all its secrets. The challenging combat, stunning world design, and cryptic lore make for a truly unforgettable adventure.

4. Baldur's Gate 3

A true return to classic RPGs. This game offers an immense amount of content, rich characters, and a story with a level of choice and consequence that few games can match. It's a perfect game to dive into if you want a long, satisfying campaign.

5. Hades

An indie masterpiece that has stood the test of time. Hades is a roguelike with a fantastic story, fast-paced combat, and a beautiful art style. The constant progression and engaging characters will have you coming back for "one more run" every time.

What are your favorite games to play during the summer? Let us know in the comments below!
